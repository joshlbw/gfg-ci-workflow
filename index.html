Are we having NMP Scheme as a Revolving Door to MPs in Singapore?

The Non-Constituency Member of Parliament (NCMP) scheme in Singapore is designed to ensure a minimum representation of opposition voices in Parliament, even if they do not win enough seats in general elections. The scheme allows the best-performing losing opposition candidates to be offered NCMP seats, thereby providing them with a platform to participate in parliamentary debates and contribute to the legislative process.
